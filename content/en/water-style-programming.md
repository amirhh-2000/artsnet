You probably learned different styles and techniques related to programming. \
But today I am going to show you a new approach. It probably won't be like the techniques and tricks you've learned before!

## Water is flowing
What I mean by this is that if the water stays in one place, it becomes stagnant and useless.
In order to stay fresh, it must be current every moment. Or in other words, **change!**\
So, try to keep your knowledge up to date. Never settle for a certain method or trick that you learned in the past. always be learning. everyday! This habit may cost you money, but the result is worth it!
